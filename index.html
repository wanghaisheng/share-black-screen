How It Works

ZenScreen helps you maintain focus by creating a distraction-free environment through its black screen overlay. Here's how to use it effectively:

  1. Activate Black Screen - Click the "Activate Black Screen" button or press the spacebar to toggle the black overlay.
  2. Adjust Opacity - Use the slider to find your optimal darkness level for eye comfort.
  3. Set a Timer - Choose a preset time for focused work sessions, and the black screen will automatically activate.
  4. Use Keyboard Shortcuts - Control the tool quickly with keyboard shortcuts for a seamless experience.

The black screen helps reduce visual distractions, allowing you to focus on your thoughts or listen without visual interruptions. It's also ideal for reducing eye strain during breaks.
